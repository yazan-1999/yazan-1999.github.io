Billing Details

DELIVERY

The ordered products will be delivered to the address indicated in the order by couriers. Payment will be made in cash at delivery for individuals and legal entities and only by bank transfer for legal entities.

Delivery time and cost The general delivery term within Chisinau is up to 3 calendar days, the delivery cost being 100 lei. The courier fee will be paid only once, no matter how many products you have in order. The courier will notify you by phone of the day and time of delivery. Payment for the package is made upon delivery to the courier.
